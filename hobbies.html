My father's Hobbies

Sand Collection

My father for almost 20 years collected sand from beaches located all over the globe. Every time I was inviting my friends over they were always fascinated to see my father's wonder wall with all little labeled jars. From the white and fine sand of Maldives to the black volcanic sand of Stromboli, my father has this and more. Of course and unfortunately, he did not have the opportunity to visit and collect the sand from this stunning places of the world. All my father's friends and co-workers knew about this passione and after a trip were always bringing a bit of sand from their destinations.

Singing

My father loves singing. And I admit he is pretty good too. I remember how we were spending our evening few years ago. My father had found a a karaoke software for the old Windows95. We were passing hours singing out loud. He also performed few times in front of an audience. Well, he performed mostly the holiday resorts stages were we were spending few weeks each year during summer. Still, people always seems to appreciate his voice. The last time he performed was at the wedding of his boss. He singed “My Way” of Frank Sinatra. I always get emotional when I hear him singing that song.

Dancing

When the music starts playing my father is the first that starts moving his body. He loves any patners dance, from salsa to the passionate argentine Tango. Many times, especially during festivals or on special occasions my father asked me to go dance with him. I always denied, maybe to shy to dance in front of all people. I a bit regret those moment knowing that it would make my father extremely happy.

Technology

When new technological devices were launched to the market my father got crazy for them. He started buying different household appliances to synchronized lights, to fountain speakers. The more absurd it was, more was the chance that he would buy it. Last Christmas he purchased Alexa, the virtual assistant of Amazon. You had to see his face when he showed me how he could turn on and off the tree lights just asking the device to do so. Similar to a child when receives a new lego set.
