My father's Life

Short biography

My father Marcello was born on January 19, 1964. He grew up in a small town near Pesaro, Italy. As my grandmother always says to me, he was a good and kind child. He was always helping his parents and loved to spend time listening to his grandmother. Growing up his behave changed, he started skipping school very often and ended up not even finishing secondary school. Despite the very short educational career, he had been great in every job he did. During his first seasonal job he met my mother. They dated for few months but then things did not go too well, they broke up soon after. Few years passed and they started to see each other again. They finally decided to get marry on the 11th October 1991. My father really wanted to have a child and, in 1995 he realised his dream. However, soon after my birth, he had few down moments. He started suffering of depression and started gaining a lot of weight. In 1997 things got better. He started gym, quit smoking and dedicated most of his time to me and to the new born, my brother Lorenzo.
