About me

My name is Deborah and I am originally from Cattolica, a little gem of the Adriatic coast in Italy. In 2015, after high school, I decided to leave my nest and fly to Dublin. For who is planning to visit Dublin, you better watch out! After one or two pints of Guinness in one of its traditional pubs (they still have live music!) you could find yourself dancing with unknown friends...or with your life soul mate. You can now imagine why, after four years, I am still leaving in the green land.
I am really easy-going and positive person. I like to do sports and keep myself active as well as chill-out with friends watching movies and eating biscuits (my weakness spot).
This year, though, things will be different. I had the courage to come back to college to study Computer Science. My hope is that, in 4 years from now, I will look back and say: "It has been hard, but come back to college was the best decision of my life. Congratulations for your degree".
