My father's Job

The career of my father was quite linear, starting from seasonal jobs to be one important member of “Fico World”. He does not have any special title or degree but his ability to empathize with other people and his organizational skills brought him to rump up pretty fast.

My father's career.

  • 1979
  • 1984
  • 1991
  • 1995
  • 2002
  • 2014
1969

First job, on the beach

Marcello started his first job at the age of 13. He was a beachboy and was helping the owner of the beach to maintain the area clean and to bring the clients to the rented chairs and umbrellas. Summers in my seaside village are great. A lot of tourists are coming in Cattolica to spend few weeks of their holidays and enjoy the sun and the good spirit of the inhabitants. Becuase of the presence of many german tourist my father had also the opportunity to learn a new language.

1984

Barman at the famous "Bar Peledo"

After few seasonal jobs in hotels and restaurants he started working as a barman in one of the central bars of Cattolica. In “Bar Peledo” my father learned the meaning of good friendship and how to make fabulous cocktails. He still in contact with all the team. Last year they also went for a dinner together.

1991

Clerk for "Coop" supermarket

My father was already in relationship with my mother (they got married the year after) and they started working in a near Supermarket chain called Coop. In Coop my father showed good organizational and technical skills and after few years he got promoted to work in Coop head office.

1995

Promotion in Ravenna, Italy

The year of my birth my father moved to Ravenna, a city distant one hour by car from our home. I never fully understood what exactly his role was. I had hard times in kindergarten to answer the question "what is your father's job?". I had to ask my father what exactly his job was about for the sake of this website. As I understood, he was following the new openings, managing the renovations and organizing the stock of the supermarket. What I am sure of is that he was always traveling around Italy and most of the time he was bringing delicious souvenirs.

2002

New office in Bologna

He moved in the head office of Coop in Bologna. The city was still quite far from our home, but this never bothered my father. He once said to me that he always wanted to work and travel at the same time and for him have to drive one hour every day is a good compromise. The job in Bologna was very similar to the one in Ravenna, but the best was yet to come.

2014

Fico World

In 2014 my father has been hired in the project “Fico world”, the world's largest agri-food park. The park, situated in Bologna, host factory of one of the major Italian food brands, and give the opportunity to the visitors to explore how crops grow, how factories use them to make traditional products and taste the final result in the different restaurants around the park. My father has been involved since the start in all the stages of the project. His main role was to inform the Italian food and drink companies about the park and to convince them to bring their products in the park.
